Guidelines What is PetTracker app? PetTracker is an app that can detect the location of our pet with our Pet Tracker device. This application can also track the pet’s routine such as their daily activity and diet intake for their pet. How to use PetTracker app?
  • Create an account to access to the application
  • If you already have an account, sign in and you will able to use the application
  • Connect your PetTracker Device with your account for connect with your pet information
 How to connect PetTracker Device?
  • - Turn on the PetTracker device and make sure the device is ready to pair
  • - Go to the ‘Manage Device’ on the home page and follow the simple steps-by-steps
  • - Now you are ready to use the device.
